Easy No Fail Pizza Dough

Prep time:
Cook time:
Yields:
Category: Main-course

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups Flour (390g)
  • 2 tbsp oil (30ml)
  • 1 cup warm water (250ml)
  • 2 tsp sugar (10ml)
  • 1 tsp yeast (5ml)
  • pinch salt (1/2 tsp)

Instructions

  1. Mix water, flour, oil, yeast and sugar. Beat in the mixer to get it combined. (2min)
  2. Add the salt.
  3. Put mixer going for 5 min.
  4. Put dough into a bowl greased with oil & let it rest for 1 hr.
